Punk Pad

I grew up on Green Day; their energy struck a chord that left me obsessed with writing songs of my own. But let’s be real: sometimes there’s, "No time for motivation." I wanted to write music without the self-loathing grind of digging through keys and chord progressions. Everything uses 4 chords anyway, right?

Rivers Cuomo, from Weezer, famously uses Python to organize his riffs and decode the DNA of the bands that inspire him. You can think of it the same way The Beatles took rock and roll from Chuck Berry and turned it into melodic power pop. I built Punk Pad with a similar scientific method in mind. Punk Pad handles the technical stuff like progressions, keys, and tempos so you can focus on the angst. Turn your raw ideas into finished songs. Check it out on GitHub and start writing!
